RugbyRecruit Ireland

What to expect?

RugbyRecruit Ireland is a website that is used, throughout Ireland, to help both players and managers across all grades of rugby. We are here to help players showcase their playing style and abilities, and we are also used to help coaches recruit players for their team. RugbyRecruit Ireland has a large database of players to choose from and gives players opportunities they might never recieve without our help.

Welcome to RugbyRecruit Ireland

This is an informational site as part of a project given by Waterford institute of technology. This is a non-profit site and ideas are purely for project purposes only. This website was designed to help users obtain notice on their abilities in the game of rugby. Nowadays, players sturggle to get the notice they feel they deserve. This site is aimed at those players, and we give them the opportunity to showcase their abilities to many clubs, colleges, and possibly provincial coaches.

RugbyRecruit Ireland gives rugby players of all ages a fantastic opportunity to be seen and scouted by coaches from all over Ireland. The site really is ahead of its time. Joe Smith
